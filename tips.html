Farming & Business Tips

1. Diversify Your Crops

Avoid relying on a single crop. Growing a variety reduces risks from pests, diseases, and market changes.

2. Use Modern Irrigation

Invest in drip irrigation or water-saving methods to increase efficiency and reduce waste.

3. Focus on Quality

High-quality produce attracts loyal customers and can be sold at better prices.

4. Market Online

Leverage digital platforms like Lesotho Hub to connect with new clients and expand your business reach.

5. Keep Records

Maintain simple records of costs, sales, and production. This helps track progress and plan for growth.

6. Sustainable Practices

Adopt eco-friendly farming methods to preserve soil health and ensure long-term productivity.
